## Research Interests and Experience

* Spatial data science and spatial databases
  * Crisp (i.e., determinate, sharply bounded) spatial objects in the two-dimensional space
  * Uncertain (i.e., indeterminate, vague, fuzzy) spatial objects in the two-dimensional space
  * Topological relationships (e.g., contains, inside, intersects)
  * Spatial indexing
  * Spatial decision support systems (e.g., fuzzy logic applied to spatial data)
  * Spatial reasoning and inference
* Data warehousing
  * Geographic and spatial data warehouses
  * OLAP and Spatial OLAP tools
  * Indexing techniques for data warehouses
* Geographic information systems (GIS)
* Big (spatial) data
* Extensible database systems and Abstract Data Types
* Emerging memories (e.g., flash-based solid state drives and non-volatile main memory)
